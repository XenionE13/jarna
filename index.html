MIRËSEVINI TE JARNA

A small family restaurant on Rruga Fortuzi.

Jarna serves traditional Albanian dishes — qofte, byrek, tavë kosi and fërgesë — the way they're cooked at home. Everything is made fresh each morning, from recipes that never needed changing. No reinvention here: just a quiet courtyard, a few tables under the vine, and the food we grew up eating, served the way our mothers served it.
